Welcome to Gadchiroli Forest Department

The Gadchiroli Forest Department works tirelessly for the conservation, protection, and sustainable management of forests and wildlife in the Gadchiroli district of Maharashtra. Known for its rich biodiversity, dense forests, and tribal communities, the department focuses on eco-tourism, forest produce management, afforestation programs, and wildlife protection.
