Our Mission & Work

The Maharashtra Forest Department, Gadchiroli, plays a crucial role in conserving the dense forests and diverse wildlife of the region. The district is home to over 75% forest cover, rich tribal culture, and invaluable medicinal flora. Key initiatives include:

  • Afforestation and forest protection programs
  • Sustainable management of minor forest produce
  • Eco-tourism development
  • Wildlife conservation and anti-poaching activities
  • Community awareness and participation drives
